Features

CashWise makes managing money simple

Track your expenses, set budgets, and stay in control of your personal finances, all in one easy-to-use app. No complicated features, no clutter. Just the essentials you need.

Cash Book

Record daily income and expenses.

Helps you gain a clear view of your total money flow and current balance in real time.

Spending

Log and categorize specific spending habits.

Helps you identify exactly where your money is going so you can cut back on unnecessary costs.

Savings

Set financial goals with specific deadlines.

Helps you stay disciplined and track your progress toward buying the things that matter most.

Recurring Expense

Manage bills and repeating payments.

Helps you stay ahead of fixed costs and ensures you never miss a subscription or utility deadline.

Budget

Set spending limits by category.

Helps you prevent overspending by giving you a clear stop sign for different parts of your lifestyle.
